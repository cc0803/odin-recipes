Curry

Description

This dish originated in India and is a staple in most indian restaurants.

Being quite easy to make this dish takes about 30 minutes with 5 minutes of preparation, but is still very flavourful. We recomend eating this dish with eather rice or Naanbread.

Ingredients

  • 200g Lentos
  • 400ml vegetable stock
  • garlic
  • 1 chilly
  • ginger
  • 1 cane Coconutmilk
  • Koriander
  • Your favourite Indian spices
  • 200g caned tomatoes (optional)
  • Your favourite vegetables like Eggplant or Carrots (optional)

Steps

  1. finely dice the ginger, garlic and chilly. Depending on how hot you like your dish take out the chilly seeds first.
  2. Optional: Dice your Vegetables in easely eatable pieces.
  3. Heat a large Pan or optimally a Wok on high heat. Put in some high heat vegetable oil and everything you diced. Season it with a little bit of salt and pepper.
  4. After glacing everything add your Spices and toast them for 30 seconds to a minute. Be caution to not burn them.
  5. Know add your Lentos and vegetable stock.
    6. Close the lit and let everything cook till the Lentos are almost finished.
  6. Finally add your Coconutmilk and let everything cook an medium heat for another 15 minutes. Optionally you could also add your tomatoes add this step.
  7. Decorate your Curry with some Koriander and serve with Rice or Naan.
