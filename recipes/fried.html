Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a classic, cheap and easy east Asian dish

This dish takes just a few Minutes (Depending on your additions to the recipe). Additionally this recipe includes just some basic building blogs for this dish, so you can make any additions to your liking or leave out parts you don't like.

Ingredients

  • Rice It tastes the best if it is a day old
  • Peas
  • Pak Choi
  • Tofu
  • Carrots
  • Chillies
  • Garlic
  • Spring onions
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Finely dice your Carrots, Garlic, Chillies, Spring Onions, Tofu and roughly choup your Pak Choi.
  2. Give all your all of this into a Wok on high heat with some Vegetable oil and the Peas
  3. After evrything is cooked add your Rice and some soy sauce, be careful not to you to much or this could turn out way to salty
  4. Another few Minutes and your Rice is ready to go
